## What it will do

Install the plugin, paste in an API key, and choose what to gate — the whole site, specific pages
and posts, or individual WooCommerce products. The plugin handles opening the session, rendering
the gate, and verifying the callback signature for you.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Gate anything" icon="file-lines">
    Whole site, selected pages/posts, a category, or WooCommerce products.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Set the age" icon="calendar">
    Choose the minimum age per rule — 16, 18, 21, or a custom value.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Shortcode & block" icon="code">
    Drop a gate anywhere with a shortcode or a Gutenberg block.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Signature handled" icon="shield-check">

  </Card>
